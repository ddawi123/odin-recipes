Lasagna Recipe:

Description:

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of broad, flat pasta sheets layered with fillings such as meat sauce (ragù), béchamel sauce, cheese (like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and vegetables, then baked in an oven until the cheese is melted and the dish is hot. The word "lasagna" can refer to both the wide, flat noodle itself and the layered, baked dish.

Ingredients:

  • Meat - italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Crushed tomato can
  • Sugar, spices, salt and pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmasan, mozzarella, and ricotta
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
